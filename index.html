Welcome to SAEAWU

The South African Economic and Allied Workers Union (SAEAWU) is dedicated to protecting the rights of workers in all sectors. We believe that every worker deserves fair wages, safe conditions, and strong representation.

Our Vision

To be a leading voice in shaping a fair and equitable labour system that uplifts workers and transforms workplaces across South Africa.

Why Join SAEAWU?

  • Dedicated support and legal representation
  • Advocacy for improved working conditions
  • Campaigns for employment opportunities and economic justice
  • Educational workshops and training programs
